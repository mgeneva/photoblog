I wanted to go on a short holiday somewhere in Europe with my father, so I pitched a few ideas to him. When I suggested Porto, his reaction was, “Porto? What on earth is there to do in Porto?”

As it turns out - quite a lot.

![Azulejos]({{ "" | relative_url }}/assets/images/Porto1.png)

We admired many azulejos, walked across the bridge designed by Eiffel’s partner, had dinner by the Doumo River, visited the majestic cathedral and several beautiful churches, went on a port wine tasting tour, explored the historic stock exchange building, and enjoyed eating pastéis almost every day.

![Doumo River]({{ "" | relative_url }}/assets/images/Porto2.png)

My father had actually been to Porto before, but only for work and never on the historic side of the city, so it was a revelation for him. Many years later, he returned to Porto to sightsee with my mother and sister.
